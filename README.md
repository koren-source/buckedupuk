# Bucked Up UK — Wholesale Landing Page

## What This Is
A landing page to acquire UK gym wholesale partners for Bucked Up (DAS Labs). The page drives cold outreach traffic (email/DM) through a persuasion sequence that builds brand credibility and converts visitors into wholesale applicants.

## Target Audience
UK gym owners, gym operators, gym café managers.

## Hero Product
**Bucked Up Protein Soda** — 25g whey protein isolate in a lightly carbonated can. Arnold Expo Best Innovation Winner. Novel product the UK market hasn't seen. 800K+ cans sold in first 60 days.

## Page Flow
1. **Hero** — Protein Soda cans + wholesale inquiry form (side by side)
2. **Authority Stats** — #1 pre-workout in USA, 100K+ retail doors, $280M valuation, etc.
3. **Protein Soda Deep Dive** — Features, macros, gym business case
4. **Full Product Range** — Energy drinks, pre-workouts (Original, Woke AF, Mother Bucker, Black Ant)
5. **Wholesale Offer** — Buy 5 get 6th free (pre-workout), free case with every 6 (Protein Soda)
6. **Partner Benefits** — POS displays, marketing support, dedicated sales rep
7. **Social Proof** — Retail logos, UK distributors, leadership credibility, digital stats
8. **Final CTA** — Apply for wholesale pricing

## Conversion Mechanics
- Lead capture form on-page (hero section) → webhook to CRM → redirect to Bucked Up's wholesale application
- All CTAs smooth-scroll to the hero form
- UTM tracking on all outbound links
